Background

This is a personal project that has come up with an idea with a new brand of video platform mobile apps that communicate actively with creators and small entertainment agencies who want to unleash their talents.

What I did:

UX/UI Design

Design
Wireframe

And there was a restriction that, at the request of the advertising agency, advertising banners should be fixed to the top, and additional banner ads to the bottom.

  1. Content-centric UI

    2. Configures the creator's image and image so that they can be visually distinguished using the margins

  2. A vibrant design in brand color

    3. Enhance your brand image by using brand colors that represent services for each of your key content configurations

  3. Configures predictably with minimal movement

    4. Designed to minimize user physical effort in menu movement with lower navigation

Design Concept

In order to show the creativity and unique atmosphere, I used high-split gradations to pursue a stylish yet simple design.

Outcome

Information was provided in text to highlight the video image. I wanted to minimize visual interference by dividing information into margins. And I sought visual comfort by not using color but by separating information with the strength of text.
